# LLM Health Safety Research

Empirical research examining safety-critical behaviors of large language models 
in clinical settings, focusing on bias, fairness, and over-reliance.

## Projects

### Bias & Fairness in LLM Clinical Recommendations
Investigating whether LLM-generated clinical recommendation systems produce 
biased outputs across patient demographic groups. Submitted to RecSys 2026.

**Status:** Under review. Code and results will be released upon publication.

### LLM Over-Reliance in Health Decision-Making
Controlled experiment testing whether LLM-generated explanations improve 
human health risk decisions or induce over-reliance among clinicians and patients.

**Status:** In progress.
